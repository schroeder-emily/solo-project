Welcome to your dashboard! Proper training for boxing is imperative. Here are a few guidelines to keep you on track:

  • Engage in a boxing class or sparring five times a week
  • Practice Yoga at least once a week for improved balance
  • Commit to two days of strength training. This should be on a day you box, but with several hours in between
  • Jumprope at least 20 minutes a day
  • REST! Take at least two rest days a week. On your rest day, you can go on a walk or engage in something very light

How to use this dashboard?

  • Click the "add a new workout" in the top right corner
  • Enter the information
  • Keep track of the workouts you have planned for the week
  • Click "finish" when you have kicked some ass
